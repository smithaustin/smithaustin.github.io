About

Darren ‘Smitty’ Smith was born and raised on Vancouver Island. He got his start in the construction business as a young man, gaining experience with several builders, and eventually landed an apprenticeship, completed in 1994, which earned him a Red Seal carpentry certification.\n

In 1996 he struck out on his own, incorporating ‘Smitty Construction’, a company that has grown steadily since then and which has gained a rock-solid reputation of excellence.

Specializing in building Custom Homes, ‘Smitty’ and his team focus strongly on quality of workmanship, clarity of communication, and reliability of all aspects of your project.
